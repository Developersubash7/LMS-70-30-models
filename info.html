70/30 Experiential LMS Model

A definitive Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for transforming product-cum-service field technicians. Moving beyond traditional classrooms to real-site mastery in waterproofing, crack repair, and protective coatings.

The 70/30 Training Philosophy

Traditional technical training fails because construction chemicals require tactile understanding. Our global benchmarked model shifts the focus dramatically. We dedicate 30% to LMS Theory (product science, safety protocols, video case studies) and 70% to Experiential Field Practice.

This visualization illustrates the core allocation of learning hours. Technicians spend the majority of their time on live sites, handling actual materials under the supervision of Master Trainers.

Competency Assessment

Skill assessment is quantified before and after the 70/30 module. We evaluate technicians across five core operational pillars critical to construction chemical application.

  • ▶ Surface Diagnostics: Identifying substrate issues accurately.
  • ▶ Mixing Ratios: Precision in multi-component chemical blending.
  • ▶ Application Tech: Brush, roller, or trowel proficiency.
  • ▶ Site Safety: Adherence to PPE and hazardous material handling.

Certification Hierarchy

Progression requires passing rigorous field evaluations and LMS assessments. This structure ensures a clear career path and guarantees service quality to the end customer.
